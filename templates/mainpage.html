WAR! (the card game)

How To Play (I Declare War)

  1. A deck of cards has been split between you and a CPU player.
  2. Each player will play a card. The highest card WINS.
  3. If the players tie, the each player puts down 3 cards. The player with the highest card WINS. (Keep doing this until the tie is broken). This is known as WAR.
  4. When a players deck reaches 0, their hold pile becomes their new deck.
  5. The game is over when one player has 0 cards both in their hold pile and deck OR when WAR occurs and a player cannot finish.

How To Play (Card Roulette)

  1. A deck of cards has been split between you and a CPU player
  2. Press Play Move. You and the CPU will choose a card from the deck at random.
  3. The player with the highest value card wins. If the player tie, both will drop 3 more cards. The player with the highest card WINS.
  4. After going through the entire deck, the player with the most cards, WINS.

How To Play (Cool Version)

  1. Start the Game by clicking a card.
  2. Like the first 2 variations, a deck of cards has been shuffled and split in half between you and a CPU player
  3. You will be shown the first 2 cards.
  4. You can choose from the 2 cards which card you want to send out. The CPU will do the same.
  5. The player with the highest card WINS!
  6. The cards will go into the winning players hold pile.
