WAR! (the card game)

How To Play

  1. A deck of cards has been split between you and a CPU player.
  2. Each player will play a card. The highest card WINS.
  3. If the players tie, the each player puts down 3 cards. The player with the highest card WINS. (Keep doing this until the tie is broken)
  4. The game is over when one player has no more cards.
